# Maintenance Notice – 4/17, 12 A.M (UTC-7)

Dear Commanders,

Headquarters will carry out maintenance on 4/17, 12:00 A.M. (UTC-7) for approximately 8 hours. Any change in the update progress will be announced on our Facebook / X pages. During the maintenance period, our servers will be unavailable.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.

**Target Servers**  
All servers on Android and iOS

**List of New Contents**

**New Events**  
_Available for a limited-time between 4/17 – 5/1, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Vacation Lane – Beachside Brilliance (During the event, play event stages to collect event PT to earn special rewards, imcluding the limted collab characters.)  
※During the event, sortie to event stages to earn event PT and exchange them for rewards including the limited character, Tsukushi.  
※During the event, accumulate PT to earn rewards including the limited character Patty.  
※The event stages have Battle Mode and Story Mode:  
\[Battle Mode\] Participate to collect event Pts to earn special rewards. The EX stage will be unlocked after finishing the SP stage.  
\[Story Mode\] Complete the story mode to get Patty’s augment module, Hamburger.

The Fox Spirit’s Retreat (Clear Main Campaign stages after Chapter 3 or event stages (EX not included) to earn Trust. If Shizuku is afloat in a fleet that defeats the Boss of a stage that grants Trust, you will gain twice as much Trust. Complete milestone missions by accumulating enough Trust to have Shizuku permanently join your fleet. Continue earning Trust to obtain Shizuku up to 3 more times.  
※Note: After this event ends, if any NPC characters remain in your dock, the game will require an online update upon re-entry. After the update, those NPC characters will be removed.)

Watermelon Splitter (A new challenge stage unlocks every day. Clear them all to get great rewards.)

Beachside Brilliance ~Venus~ (Play through the stories to unlock rewards, including limited gear skins.)

Manjuu Hot Springs (Shipgirls bathing in the hot springs will restore Morale more quickly. Play through Vacation Lane – Beachside Brilliance stages to get 4 Hot Springs Passes. Use them to unlock additional slots in the hot springs facility.)

Rental Outfits (Log in during the event period to earn 3 rental outfit vouchers, which can be used on the following skins: Argus – Dignified Swimsuit Training (L2D+), Elise – One Divine Afternoon (Dynamic), Shandy – An Enchanting Sip (Dynamic).)

_Available for a limited-time between 4/17 – 5/6, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Rapid Training Campaign (During the campaign, log in to obtain Skill Books and 20 Wisdom Cubes. Each day during the campaign, you will be able to finish 10 classes immediately when using Skill Books in the Tactical Class.)
