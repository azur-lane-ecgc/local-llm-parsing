# Maintenance Notice – 12/4, 12 A.M (UTC-7)

Dear Commanders,

Headquarters will carry out maintenance on 12/4, 12:00 A.M. (UTC-7) for approximately 6 hours. Any change in the update progress will be announced on our Facebook / X pages. During the maintenance period, our servers will be unavailable.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.

**Target Servers**  
All servers on Android and iOS

**List of New Contents**

**New Events**  
_Available for a limited-time between 12/4/2025 – 2/2/2026, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Popularity Poll 2025 (Complete event missions to collect ballots and use them to vote for your favorite shipgirls and receive rewards based on how many votes you have cast.)  
※The Popularity Poll 2025 will have two types of votes: the regular Main Votes (7 rounds including Qualifiers, Group A-D, Repechage, Finals) and the Special Votes (3 rounds including Special Votes I-III).  
※All Commanders who have reached Commander Level 25 are able to obtain Main Vote ballots by completing daily missions and one-time missions and obtain Special Votes ballots by logging daily.  
※Excluding shipgirls already in the Hall of Fame (Amagi, Laffey, Bismarck, New Jersey, Prinz Eugen, Friedrich der Große, Shinano, Ägir, Kearsarge), all shipgirls available in the game before November 23rd, 2025 are eligible to participate. Collaboration characters (excluding blueprint ships), µ-rigging, Type II rigging, as well as alternate versions of shipgirls cannot participate in the poll, but META and Little shipgirls can be voted on in the special vote rounds.  
※Characters that are newly added to the Hall of Fame will receive new stories and limited portrait frames. The commemorative furniture will be available between 2/3/2026 – 2/7/2026, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7) after the Popularity Poll 2025 ends.

_Available for a limited-time between 12/4/2025 – 1/7/2026, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Memories in Echo (Clear Main Campaign stages after Chapter 3 to earn “Blue Light”. Accumulate “Blue Light” to gradually unlock the event story. Complete it to get limited gear “Blue Sea Mystery”.)

_Available for a limited-time between 12/4 – 12/10, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Emergency Module Development (During the event, Emergency Module Development can be challenged once per day to obtain Augment Module Core, Augment Module Stone, and other rewards. In addition, Augment Module Core and Augment Module Stone could be claimed by clearing cumulative missions of Emergency Module Development.)  
※The quick battle option will be unlocked after completing the raid with an S rating.
