# Maintenance Notice – 11/20, 12 A.M (UTC-7)

Dear Commanders,

Headquarters will carry out maintenance on 11/20, 12:00 A.M. (UTC-7) for approximately 8 hours. Any change in the update progress will be announced on our Facebook / X pages. During the maintenance period, our servers will be unavailable.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.

**Target Servers**  
All servers on Android and iOS

**List of New Contents**

**New Events**  
_Available for a limited-time between 11/20 – 12/3, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
DATE A LANE (During the event, complete Joint Operation-type event stages to earn Contribution PT and get special rewards.)

\[Event Stages\]  
※This event includes six special event stages (SECTOR-1 to SECTOR 6) These special stages combine the characteristics of Joint Operation and Coalition Battle. Fully clear each stage and seize full control to advance the operation (reducing the enemy control bar to 0%).  
※Once the enemy control bar reaches 0% for any event stage, all participating Commanders will receive the “full control rewards” of that stage via in-game mail. Commanders who did not participate before the enemy control bar hits 0% will receive a special mission; completing it will grant the same rewards.

\[Upgrade Airship\]  
※Complete the event missions to receive Fraxinus AL Improvement Data and upgrade Tech Modules. Complete all Tech Modules upgrade to get Nightmare Labyrinth Medal.

\[Reward\]  
※During the event, sortie to event stages to earn event PT and exchange them for rewards including the limited character Kaguya Yamai・Yuzuru Yamai.  
※During the event, accumulate PT to earn rewards including the limited characters Yoshino.

Nightmare Rhapsody (Clear Main Campaign stages after Chapter 3 or event stages to earn event PT. Earn bonus event PT for clearing stages with Kurumi Tokisaki in the fleet. Milestone missions will unlock over 7 days. Complete them all to have Kurumi Tokisaki permanently join your fleet. You can earn up to 3 additional copies of Kurumi Tokisaki by accumulating more event PT.)

Side Stories (Read the side stories to get limited collab gear skins. Complete all side stories to get the limited collab auxilary, Michael.)

Rental Outfits (Log in during the event period to earn 3 rental outfit vouchers, which can be used on the following skins: Tohka Yatogami – Break Time (Dynamic), Kurumi Tokisaki – Twin Rose (Dynamic), Kotori Itsuka – Waterside Goddess (Dynamic).)

_Available for a limited-time between 11/20 – 12/10, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Rapid Training Campaign (During the campaign, log in to obtain Skill Books and 20 Wisdom Cubes. Each day during the campaign, you will be able to finish 10 classes immediately when using Skill Books in the Tactical Class.)

_Available for a limited-time between 12/1/2025 – 1/31/2026, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_  
Cruise Missions (During the campaign season, accumulate progress points to earn rewards.)  
※Cruise Missions Rewards will consist of Regular Rewards and Fair Winds Rewards. Regular Rewards will be available to all while the Fair Winds Rewards will require the purchase of Fair Winds Cruise Pass.  
※During the campaign season, complete missions and accumulate progress points to get Regular Rewards including the limited character Boise META and Specialized Bulin Custom MKIII.  
※Purchasing the Fair Winds Cruise Pass for $9.99 will grant 1,500 progress points immediately. Additionally, accumulate progress points to get Fair Winds Rewards including I-13’s limited outfit “After-School Observations” and Battle UI.  
※The campaign missions, progress points, Fair Winds Cruise Pass and rewards will only be available during the campaign season.
