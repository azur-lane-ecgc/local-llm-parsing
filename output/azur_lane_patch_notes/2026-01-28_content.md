# Maintenance Notice – 1/29, 12 A.M (UTC-7)

Dear Commanders,

Headquarters will carry out maintenance on 1/29, 12:00 A.M. (UTC-7) for approximately 6 hours. Any change in the update progress will be announced on our Facebook / X pages. During the maintenance period, our servers will be unavailable.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.

**Target Servers**  
All servers on Android and iOS

_Available for a limited-time between 2/1/2026 – 3/31/2026, 11:59 P.M. (UTC-7)_

※During the campaign season, complete missions and accumulate progress points to get Regular Rewards including the limited character Carabiniere META and Specialized Bulin Custom MKIII.  
※Purchasing the Fair Winds Cruise Pass for $9.99 will grant 1,500 progress points immediately. Additionally, accumulate progress points to get Fair Winds Rewards including Z47’s limited outfit “Batter ★ Home Run” and Battle UI (Ancient Dynasty).  
※The campaign missions, progress points, Fair Winds Cruise Pass and rewards will only be available during the campaign season.
