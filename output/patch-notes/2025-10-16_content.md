# Maintenance Notice – 10/16, 12 A.M (UTC-7)

Dear Commanders,

Headquarters will carry out maintenance on 10/16, 12:00 A.M. (UTC-7) for approximately 8 hours. Any change in the update progress will be announced on our Facebook / X pages. During the maintenance period, our servers will be unavailable.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused during this period.

**Target Servers**  
All servers on Android and iOS
