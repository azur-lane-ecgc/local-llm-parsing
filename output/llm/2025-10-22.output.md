# 2025-10-22

## Ships

- **Pearl**: Available in the event construction pool “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” (rate‑up) and can be obtained from the PT shop or as a reward for accumulated event PT.
- **Adventure**: Earned as a reward of accumulated PT during the “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” event.
- **Amity**: Rate‑up in both event construction pools 1 and 2; also obtainable via PT shop exchange.
- **Dolphin**: Rewarded for accumulated event PT during the “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” event.
- **Queen Anne’s Revenge**: Rate‑up in the event construction pool 1 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád”.
- **Lyme**: Rate‑up in the event construction pool 1 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád”.
- **Royal James**: Rate‑up in the event construction pool 1 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád”.
- **Ganj‑i‑Sawai**: Available in event construction pools 1 and 2 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” / “Tempesta and the Sleeping Sea”.
- **Fancy**: Available in event construction pools 1 and 2 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” / “Tempesta and the Sleeping Sea”.
- **Portsmouth Adventure**: Rate‑up in event construction pools 1 and 2 for “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” / “Tempesta and the Sleeping Sea”.
- **Uranami**: Available for purchase in the Core Data shop (Mo.) after 11/1.

## Equipment

- **Invitation to Islas de Libertád**: Limited gear obtainable by completing event stories in the “Tempesta and Islas de Libertád” event.
- **Rum Keg**: Limited gear obtainable by clearing all rounds of the “Onward! Manjuu Pirates! II” event.
